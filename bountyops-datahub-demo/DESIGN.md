## Style Prompt

Quiet technical product demo on a warm white operations canvas. Slack conversation and DataHub metadata are presented as crisp working surfaces, while oversized editorial typography gives the video a clear narrative spine. Motion is precise and directional, suggesting evidence moving from catalog to decision.

## Colors

- Canvas: #F7F6F3
- Ink: #1D1C1D
- Slack aubergine: #4A154B
- DataHub green: #007A5A
- Signal yellow: #ECB22E
- Rule gray: #D9D5D9

## Typography

- Headline and UI: Inter
- Data, URNs, and status labels: JetBrains Mono
- Use 900 weight for statements and 400 weight for explanatory copy.

## Motion

- Primary transition: 0.5-second horizontal push with power3.inOut.
- Entrances are staggered, directional, and limited to opacity plus transforms.
- Data rows reveal in reading order; lineage grows left to right.

## What NOT to Do

- No gradients, decorative blobs, or neon effects.
- No fake Devpost or submission screens.
- No tiny web-sized text.
- No metadata mutations represented as completed actions.
- No unrelated bounty or Qwen footage reused as DataHub evidence.
